I’d not think twice

With all the ice 

To hike, to climb,

To give advice 

To walk and wander,

Wayward still

O’er fjords

And through the Chill. 

  

“But stumble away,”

I’d try to say.

“Ignore the warnings, anyway.”

“There will always be a Better Day,”

“To live your life between delays.”
