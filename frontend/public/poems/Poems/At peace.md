At last at peace

And now at ease

No doubt due 

To lack of needs.


With the sun above 

And sand below,

It’s the breeze above

The undertow

Which brings our woes

Out to sea, 

And warns us 

Warmly, to abide

And stay ashore

Else for the rip tides.
